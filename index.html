About Us

Welcome to Jesus The Bread of Life Church, a place where lives are transformed through faith, worship, and the power of the Holy Spirit. We are a community of believers who stand firm in the Word of God, committed to spreading the gospel and uplifting lives through prayer, deliverance, and restoration. At JBLC, we strive to create a space where everyone feels the love of Christ and experiences His divine presence.



Our Vision

Deliverance and restoration of mankind by the preaching of the word of faith and the power of the Holy Spirit.



Our Mission

We believe in Jesus Christ as our Lord and Savior and are dedicated to sharing His love, faith, and salvation with all nations.

Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Gaspard Kazadi is the leader of Jesus The Bread of Life Church. With a passion for ministry and a heart for people, he has dedicated his life to preaching the gospel, leading with wisdom, and inspiring lives through the power of faith. His teachings focus on deliverance, restoration, and walking in divine purpose.
