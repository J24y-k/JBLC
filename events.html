Youth Conference


This is a celebration for the youth of JBLC Church, where we come together to share God's word, grow spiritually, and give praise to the Most High. It is a time of learning, fellowship, and empowerment for young believers.


Qavah 2nd Edition


Inspired by Isaiah 40:31 - "But those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength. They will soar on wings like eagles; they will run and not grow weary, they will walk and not be faint." These are days of intense prayer, seeking God’s presence, receiving divine impaction , and growing spiritually.


Crossover 2025-2026


As we transition from 2025 into 2026, we come together to give thanks, honor, and adoration to the Most High. This is a sacred time to dedicate the new year to God, knowing that the best way to start and end a year is with Him at the center of our lives.


You Are Welcome!
