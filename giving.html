The Heart of Giving

2 Corinthians 9:7 (NIV): "Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

Giving is an act of worship and faith. It is through our tithes, offerings, and donations that we honor God and support His work. We believe that every seed sown into His kingdom will bear fruit in due season.

Your generosity helps us spread the Gospel, support those in need, and grow the ministry. Whether it is your tithe, freewill offering, or a special donation, every contribution makes a difference.
